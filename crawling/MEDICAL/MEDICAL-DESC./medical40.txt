Official AIIMS@Delhi app for taking and cancelling appointments, viewing lab reports and interacting with your doctor. Note: you will need a UHID number and your mobile number registered in AIIMS database to use this app. The following features are available:1. view your patient profile2. view lab reports3. book and cancel appointments4.
