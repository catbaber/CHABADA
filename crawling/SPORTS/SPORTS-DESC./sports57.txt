GotGame is a location based mobile application which aims at creating a community of sports enthusiasts. It allows users to find similarly skilled and nearby partners to play their favourite sports with. It makes organising games much easier, by offering a complete experience, from finding similarly skilled people with similar sporting interests near you to join in an existing game, to being able to book venues online and create a game for yourself. It aims at being the next big social platform, a social medium for Sports! Use our currency, &quot;Kicks&quot; to book various sporting venues in and around the NCR region.
