This is an official Mobile Ordering application of Domino’s Pizza India and Sri Lanka. It allows you to view and use the complete menu and content for the purpose of placing an order directly at Domino’s Pizza Restaurants. The application accepts all the valid coupons introduced by Domino’s Pizza India and Sri Lanka respectively. The delivery promise of 30 minute guarantee is applicable for all orders placed through this Mobile Application. For more details please refer to information and T&amp;C section while placing your order with the mobile application. T&amp;C Apply.
